Current Paper Shape

The project is now best framed as a technical method paper: SandScout automatically mines semantic sandbox boundary candidates, synthesizes safe sentinel tasks, runs real coding agents, and scores evidence with a trace oracle and safety scanner. SSE-Bench is the output artifact, not the main contribution by itself.

Safety Posture

The artifact policy remains sentinel-only. SandScout is designed to detect and flag exploit payload indicators, credential/secret indicators, destructive commands, and product-specific vulnerability claims in task/run artifacts, while the released benchmark content avoids those categories.

Next Best Step

The strongest next experimental move is still a smaller or longer-budget tool-manifest no-note fixture that can complete normally. The strongest writing move is to expand the Introduction around the gap between behavioral hijacking benchmarks and OS/container sandbox escape benchmarks.
